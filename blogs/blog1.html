1. TRAFFIC THROUGH ORGANIC GOOGLE SEARCH

Organic traffic plays a very important role if the blog is regarding any information or value-adding niche. For this, you only have to focus on keyword research and target low competition keywords. The organic traffic will help you in the long term and will give substantial traffic as compared to any other traffic sources.

For organic traffic, the most important thing is to do keyword research which has low competition and good CPC.

Keywords should be a long-tail keyword(with 3 or more words) which will have less keyword difficulty and will be easier to rank your article on.

2. TRAFFIC THROUGH QUORA

This is one of the major sources of traffic and backlink for your website.

In the initial days when your website is new and you are not ranking on google. How will you get the traffic? You can get traffic through Quora by solving the problems of the people.

I will tell you how to drive the traffic from Quora to the website.

Some of your answers can also rank on Google from there you can get very good traffic. But remember you have to write a very optimized answer to the question. Then the probability of ranking will be high.

Here are some prerequisites:-

  1. You need a Quora account.
  2. Fill up your bio related to your website niche
  3. Follow the relevant topics on Quora
  4. Answer the queries of people related to that topic in which you can add some value.

  • Bio

    • In the above image that is from Quora, you can see how to enter a bio that must be about your experience in that particular niche.

  • Feed

    • This knows about section will manage your feed. This means it will show you the relevant questions and answers in your feed section.

  • Answers

    • In this way, you can answer the questions which will be automatically suggested to you by Quora depending upon your knowledge about section topics.

    You will have to solve the problems of the people by giving the answers and solving the queries of the people.

    You should not give direct links to your article, you will have to give a short answer and like saying that If you want detailed information do visit my blog like these things.

    You can give an anchor link to your article in that answer.

    3. TRAFFIC THROUGH PINTEREST

    The traffic through Pinterest is very targeted and is highly from the USA which has high CPC and the audience has high purchasing power.

    Pinterest has major traffic from Tier-1 countries which are an added advantage.

    Now again our favorite question,

    How to get traffic from Pinterest?

    First, you need to have a Pinterest Business Account. It is a simple process to create an existing Pinterest account to a business account.

    Then fill up your bio like what is your Pinterest account about.

    Create the boards by Different names related to your niche and pin the relevant pins on that board.

    You will also have to create your own pins to insert your link in that pins and redirect traffic to your website.

    Following are the Boards of My Pinterest account you can have Boards related to your Niche

    This is a very good way to earn high-quality traffic to your blog.

    You need to be consistent with pining. Daily 4-5 pins must be created and should be pinned daily.

    There are some websites that will help you create attractive pins daily are Canva, Adobe Spark, etc.

    4. TRAFFIC THROUGH YOUTUBE

    You can create a Youtube channel related to your website and post some video content there related to your subtopics.

    You can post a link to your blog in the video description from there also you can get a significant amount of traffic.

    These videos will also act as visual content for your blog.

    5. TRAFFIC THROUGH SOCIAL MEDIA

    This is also a very good source of traffic for your blog as it will have a permanent audience connected to you every time.

    You can post anytime anything on your social media with the article link which will drive some traffic to your blog.

    This audience of social media is fixed by following you.

    Popular social media are Facebook, Instagram, etc

    Traffic through paid Advertisement on Facebook

    You can also run ads on Facebook and can gain a good amount of traffic from ads too. If you have that initial budget you can do advertisements.
